How does this work?

So, what you can do is create a new folder in your bookmarks, and then drag each of theses links to the folder. Then, when you're on the hbonow page with all the movies, you click on a filter. This will filter out all the movies after the selected year. It will also shuffle them so you won't be biased towards movies at the beginning of the alphabet and always see a different configuration for your selection of movies!
